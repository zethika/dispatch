`handleSave` function: calls `environmentsApi.saveEnvironment(workspaceId, selectedEnvSlug, updatedEnvFile)` and `environmentsApi.saveSecretValues(workspaceId, selectedEnvSlug, updatedSecrets)`, then calls `useEnvironmentStore.getState().refreshActiveVariables(workspaceId)` if the saved environment is the active one.
